Nino

My name is Nino Panzarella. I'm at the age of 18, which I consider to be the prime of my life (so far). I'm a high school graduate, currently living in Southern California. I live in a beautiful place and I am surrounded by beautiful people. I portray all of this through my passion, which is photography. You may never meet anyone with more ambition to pursue a dream than yours truly. Although i'm still finding myself as a person and a photographer, I have my goals and they only get closer. Along with the support of my followers, I found myself somewhere I never thought I would be, and I can't wait to see what lies ahead.
